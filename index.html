Welcome to Heard Street Discovery Academy

Heard Street Discovery Academy is part of Worcester Public Schools, serving K–6 students with a focus on literacy, academic growth, and supportive classroom communities.

Heard Street Discovery Academy emphasizes reading comprehension, writing across subjects, classroom discussions, attendance, and academic growth. After-school programs are available for grades 3–6.

Programs & Focus

Structured learning for elementary students with a focus on literacy and academic growth. The school participates in district-wide goals and community activities in the South Quadrant of Worcester Public Schools.
